Our neurology service evaluates and treats disorders of the brain, spinal cord, nerves and muscles. From migraines and seizures to stroke recovery and movement disorders, our consultants combine clinical assessment with on-site imaging and EEG to reach a confident diagnosis fast.

We work closely with primary-care physicians and offer follow-up programs that prioritise quality of life for patients with long-term neurological conditions.

Common conditions we manage:

  • Migraine and chronic headache
  • Epilepsy and seizure disorders
  • Stroke rehabilitation
  • Parkinson's disease and tremor
  • Peripheral neuropathy

Our imaging department combines digital X-ray, ultrasound, and CT services with rapid reporting from on-site radiologists. Whether for a routine antenatal scan or an emergency trauma series, results are returned to your consultant within hours, not days.

All equipment is upgraded on a five-year cycle to keep dose levels low and image quality high.

  • Digital X-ray (chest, abdomen, ortho, dental)
  • Obstetric and abdominal ultrasound
  • Doppler studies
  • Contrast-enhanced CT
  • Image-guided biopsy

Our cardiology service offers preventive screening, comprehensive evaluation and ongoing management of heart and vascular conditions. The unit performs in-house ECG, echocardiography and stress testing, with referral pathways for interventional procedures.

Lifestyle counselling and structured rehabilitation are an integral part of every care plan.

  • Resting and exercise ECG
  • Transthoracic echocardiography
  • Ambulatory blood-pressure monitoring
  • Hypertension and lipid clinic
  • Cardiac rehabilitation

Our microbiology lab supports diagnosis and treatment of infectious disease across the centre and partner clinics in the region. From throat swabs to blood cultures, all assays are processed on-site with rapid antimicrobial sensitivity reporting.
