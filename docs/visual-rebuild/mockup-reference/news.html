New paediatric ward opens with expanded capacity

Lifeline has officially opened its expanded paediatric wing — adding twelve new in-patient beds, a dedicated treatment room, and a sunlit family-friendly waiting area. The expansion brings the centre's total paediatric capacity to twenty-eight beds and is part of a broader programme to make specialist care for children more accessible to families across the Gayaza catchment.

Three new specialists join our cardiology team

We're pleased to welcome three new consultants to the cardiology department this month, bringing additional clinic hours every weekday and expanded capacity for stress testing. The new appointments include a specialist in adult congenital follow-up, a hypertension lead, and a clinical nurse practitioner running the centre's anticoagulation programme.
