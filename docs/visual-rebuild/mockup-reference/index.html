About us

Welcome to Lifeline Medical Centre

For more than two decades, Lifeline Medical Centre has been the trusted choice for primary and specialist care in Gayaza. Our purpose-built facility brings together general medicine, maternity, surgery, diagnostics, and a 24-hour emergency department under one roof.

Every member of our staff — from physicians and nurses to laboratory technologists and support teams — is here for one reason: to help our patients live longer, healthier lives. We invest continuously in equipment, training, and the patient experience because care should never feel routine.

Insurance services are available and we work with major regional providers, so you can focus on getting better rather than the paperwork.
