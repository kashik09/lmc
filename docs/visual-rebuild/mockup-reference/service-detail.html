X-Ray

Digital radiography with on-site reporting

X-Ray Imaging

Our digital X-ray service produces high-resolution images at the lowest practical radiation dose, with on-site radiologist reporting available the same day. Whether your referral is for a chest film, a dental view or a post-fracture follow-up, you'll be seen, scanned and out of the department in under thirty minutes.

Studies are stored to a secure PACS, so your consultant — and any specialist they refer you to — has access to the images and reports immediately.

Walk-in appointments are accepted for routine referrals; complex or contrast-enhanced studies are scheduled in advance so that we can prepare and brief you fully.

Procedures & Studies

The most-requested study at the centre — used to assess the lungs, heart, ribs and surrounding tissue. A typical study takes under five minutes, requires no preparation, and produces a clinician's report within two hours.

Typical indications: persistent cough, suspected pneumonia, chest pain, pre-operative screening.

A standard plain-film study of the abdomen, useful for evaluating bowel obstruction, perforation, and the position of medical devices. Performed on-demand, with results available to your referring clinician the same day.

Imaging of bones and joints — from a single suspected fracture to comprehensive trauma series. We perform follow-up films during fracture healing and pre-/post-operative orthopaedic studies.

Periapical and panoramic dental imaging, supporting the dental clinic and external referrers. Low-dose digital sensors keep exposure to a minimum, particularly important for paediatric patients.

A standard chest film bundled with the centre's pre-employment medical examination. Reports are formatted for HR submission and can be issued in your name on the same day.
